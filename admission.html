Admission into RUB Programmes for the Academic Year 2024

Application Dates

The online application for College of Science and Technology, Gyalpozhing College of Information Technology, Paro College of Education, Gedu College of Business Studies, Samtse College of Education and three new programmes of Sherubtse College (Bachelor of Economics and Political Science; Bachelor of Digital Communication and Project Management; and Bachelor of Data Science and Data Analytics) will open on 1st March and close on 22nd March 2024. Students wishing to seek admission into these Colleges may apply to the respective Colleges on the aforementioned dates. The online application links will be announced by the Royal University of Bhutan.

The online application for College of Natural Resources, Jigme Namgyel Engineering College, and Sherubtse College (BSc in Physics, BSc in Chemistry, BSc in Mathematics and BSc in Life Science) will open centrally on 5th April and close on 5th May 2024.

Tuition Fees

Tuition fees for the Academic Year 2024-2025:

  • Arts and Humanities programmes: Nu. 75,457
  • Science and Engineering programmes: Nu. 93,479

The tuition fee will increase at the rate of 2% per year. The tuition is payable annually in two installments. 50% each in the beginning of each semester.

For other details, please visit the respective Colleges’ websites.

Special Scholarship in Leadership for 6 students

This scholarship will be considered for applicants for the BSc in Life Science, BSc in Mathematics, BSc in Chemistry, and BSc in Physics programmes only.

Aspiring candidates must fulfil the following criteria:

  • Be a regular student in 2023
  • Has served as the School Captain in the academic year 2023
  • Meet the minimum criteria for the programme that they would be enrolling in at Sherubtse College
  • Merit ranking will be based on aggregate of four subjects (English plus three best subjects)

Candidates who meet the above criteria should apply through email at records.sherubtse@rub.edu.bt on or before 19th April 2024. The application form for the scholarship can be downloaded from the College website: www.sherubtse.edu.bt. Your application will be acknowledged by an email. If the acknowledgement is not received by 23rd April 2024, please contact Student Record Office at 04 535341.
