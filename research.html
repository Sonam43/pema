Annual College Research Grant (ACRG)

To enhance practical research knowledge and skills, and to keep faculty members and students engaged, Sherubtse College reserves at least 1-2% of its annual budget every year to support research activities through the Annual College Research Fund (ACRF). This year, the college funded 10 research proposals under ACRF.

Publications

The college publishes journals, research reports, and conference proceedings periodically. It has its own research journal, Sherub Doenme: The Research Journal of Sherubtse College. This peer-reviewed journal is published annually in both print and online formats. It features original research papers, review articles, and short communications on multidisciplinary topics in natural and social sciences.
